Our Story

At STRIDE, we are passionate about shoes. Our journey began 5 years ago when our founder, Romeo Coliba, recognized the need for stylish and comfortable shoes that could take you from the office to a night out.

Our Mission

At STRIDE, our mission is to provide our customers with the best shoes on the market. We believe that everyone deserves to feel comfortable and confident in their footwear.

Our Team

Our team is made up of dedicated professionals who share a passion for shoes. From our designers to our customer service representatives, we are all committed to providing our customers with the best possible experience.

Our Community

At STRIDE, we believe in giving back to the community. We are committed to supporting organizations that make a difference in the lives of those in need. We believe that by working together, we can make the world a better place.
